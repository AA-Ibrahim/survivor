Welcome to Among Us

Crewmates must stay vigilant and vote the impostors out. Impostors need to "kill" all crewmates while avoiding being caught.

You are a CREWMATE.

Try to survive the night and avoid getting killed by the impostors. Stay alert to everything happening around you. An impostor could kill you without you even realizing.

To win as a crewmate:

  1. Vote out ALL impostors
  2. Collect corks scattered in these rooms: living room, kitchen, rec room, tv room, bathroom (upstairs), bathroom (basement)

Note:

  • Collecting a cork and reporting it to Alaa or Mo gives you a clue on how an impostor could kill.
  • See anything suspicious? Call a meeting. If you think you know who an impostor could be, call for an emergency meeting and everyone gathers for a discussion and vote.
